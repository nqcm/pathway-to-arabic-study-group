# اسم الجِنس الجَمعِي — The Collective Genus Noun

**Ism al-jins al-jamʿī** (اسم الجنس الجمعي) is a noun that refers to an **entire genus or category collectively** as a single concept — without counting individual members. It is the name for the whole type of thing at once.

---

## Examples

| Collective | Meaning | Singular (method) | Singular |
|---|---|---|---|
| **تُفَّاح** | apples (all/any apples) | add تَاء مَربُوطَة | **تُفَّاحَة** (one apple) |
| **شَجَر** | trees | add تَاء مَربُوطَة | **شَجَرَة** (one tree) |
| **نَخل** | palm trees | add تَاء مَربُوطَة | **نَخلَة** (one palm tree) |
| **عَرَب** | Arabs (all Arabs) | add يَاء النِّسبَة | **عَرَبِيّ** (one Arab) |
| **تُرك** | Turks | add يَاء النِّسبَة | **تُركِيّ** (one Turk) |
| **رُوم** | Romans/Byzantines | add يَاء النِّسبَة | **رُومِيّ** (one Roman) |

---

## Two Methods to Make the Singular

| Method | Used For | How |
|---|---|---|
| **Add تَاء مَربُوطَة** | Things (fruit, trees, objects) | تُفَّاح → تُفَّاحَة |
| **Add يَاء النِّسبَة** | Peoples and ethnic groups | عَرَب → عَرَبِيّ |

---

## Quranic Occurrence

**أَعرَاب** (the Bedouins) — a collective genus noun for the desert-dwelling Arab tribes. It refers to the group as a whole:

> **قَالَتِ الأَعرَابُ آمَنَّا** (Al-Ḥujurāt 49:14) — "The Bedouins (aʿrāb) said: We believe."

Singular: **أَعرَابِيّ** (one Bedouin).

---

## Modern Shifts

The word **شَعب** has developed modern connotations beyond its Quranic meaning:
- **Quranic**: شَعب (pl. شُعُوب) = a large group, a people/nation
- **Modern**: often implies "ordinary/common people" (populist sense)
- **شَعبِيَّة** as an adjective = popular/populist; as a noun = popularity
