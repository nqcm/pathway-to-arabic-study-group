# Pathway to Arabic — Study Group Notes

This site collects structured study notes for the [Pathway to Arabic](https://arabic.naveeraashraf.com) study group, led by Naveera Ashraf.

The sessions cover Quranic Arabic grammar, morphology, vocabulary, and tafseer — working through sūrahs one āyah at a time.

---

## What You Will Find Here

- **Session Notes** — detailed notes for each study session, organised by sūrah
- **Topic Notes** — atomic reference notes on individual grammar rules, morphology patterns, vocabulary roots, and Quranic concepts

## A Note on How These Notes Are Made

These notes are AI-generated from session recordings and YouTube videos, then reviewed for accuracy. They are intended as a study aid and revision reference for students of the group.

While every effort is made to preserve the content faithfully, always refer back to the original session recordings for the full context of any discussion.
