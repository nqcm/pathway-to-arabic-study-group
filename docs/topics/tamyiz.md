# Tamyiz

**Summary**: Tamyiz (تمييز) is a specifying noun used to clarify what is being counted or measured.

---

## What is Tamyiz?

A **tamyiz** (تمييز) is a noun that removes ambiguity by specifying the meaning of what comes before it. In simple terms, it answers the question: "of what?"

---

## Tamyiz in Numbers (11-99)

For numbers 11 through 99 (excluding round tens), the counted noun appears as tamyiz and follows this pattern:

- **Singular** in form
- **Mansub (accusative)** in case

Example:

> _Tis'un wa 'ishruna suratan_ - "29 surahs"

Here _suratan_ is the tamyiz: singular and mansub.
