# Oaths in Quran

Welcome to the study notes for the Oaths in Quran workshop series. This three-session workshop covers the concept of oaths (qasam) in the Quran from historical, theological, and grammatical perspectives, drawing primarily on Dr. Abdul Raheem's book *Aksam al-Qur'an* (Types of Oaths in the Quran).

## Study Sessions

- [Session 1](session-1.md) — Introduction to oaths: definitions, cross-cultural history, Quranic terminology, and three schools of thought
- [Session 2](session-2.md) — Grammar of oaths: particles, verbs, nouns of qasam, and comprehensive rules for jawāb al-qasam
- [Session 3](session-3.md) — Conditional sentences review and the combination of kasam with shart; the la-in construction
