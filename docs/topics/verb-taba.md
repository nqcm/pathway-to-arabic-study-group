# تَابَ — To Repent; Two Prepositions, Two Meanings

**تَابَ / يَتُوبُ** (root: ت-و-ب, ajwaf wāwī) = to turn, to return; used specifically for repentance and divine forgiveness.

---

## Two Critical Preposition Usages

| Construction | Direction | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **تَابَ (العَبدُ) إِلَى اللَّهِ** | Person → Allah (upward) | The person turns TO Allah in repentance |
| **تَابَ اللَّهُ عَلَى عَبدِهِ** | Allah → Person (downward) | Allah turns UPON His servant in forgiveness |

!!! important "Never Swap the Prepositions"
    - **إِلَى** (toward) = used when the servant repents — looking UP toward Allah
    - **عَلَى** (upon) = used when Allah forgives — His mercy comes DOWN upon the person
    
    Swapping these is both linguistically incorrect and theologically wrong.

---

## Derivatives

| Arabic | Pattern | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **تَوبَة** | maṣdar | repentance; the act of turning to Allah |
| **تَائِب** | ism fāʿil | one who repents (once/normally) |
| **تَوَّاب** | فَعَّال (intensive) | one who repents constantly; or: Ever-Accepting of repentance (attribute of Allah) |

---

## تَائِب vs تَوَّاب — Intensive Pattern

The **فَعَّال** pattern signals **intensity, frequency, or profession**:

| Verb | فَاعِل (once) | فَعَّال (intensive/habitual) |
|---|---|---|
| خَبَزَ (to bake) | خَابِز (one who bakes) | خَبَّاز (professional baker) |
| حَلَقَ (to cut hair) | حَالِق | حَلَّاق (barber) |
| تَابَ (to repent) | تَائِب (one who repents) | تَوَّاب (one who repents constantly) |

---

## تَوَّاب for Allah and for People

- **تَائِب** = ONLY used for people
- **تَوَّاب** = used for **both**:
  - For people: *those who repent abundantly*
  - For Allah: the name **التَّوَّاب** = the Ever-Accepting of repentance

---

## The Hadīth

> *كُلُّ ابنِ آدَمَ خَطَّاء وَخَيرُ الخَطَّائِين التَّوَّابُون*
> *"Every son of Adam is a great sinner, and the best of the great sinners are those who constantly repent."*

- **خَطَّاء** (فَعَّال) = one who makes many mistakes (not just an occasional خَاطِئ)
- **التَّوَّابُون** (فَعَّال plural) = those who repent abundantly

## Examples from the Quran

| Āyah | Construction | Type |
|---|---|---|
| **وَتُوبُوا إِلَى اللَّهِ** (various) | Person → Allah | Servant's repentance (إِلَى) |
| **تَابَ اللَّهُ عَلَيهِم** (Al-Tawbah 9:117) | Allah → Person | Allah's forgiveness (عَلَى) |
| **إِنَّ اللَّهَ هُوَ التَّوَّابُ الرَّحِيم** (various) | Allah's name | The Ever-Accepting of repentance |
