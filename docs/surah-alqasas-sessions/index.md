# At the Well of Madyan

A series studying selected āyāt from **Sūrat al-Qaṣaṣ** (سُورَة الْقَصَص), focusing on the story of Mūsā (AS) at the well of Madyan (Qasas 23–28). The lessons blend tafseer, Arabic grammar, morphology, and cultural context, using Dr. Abdur Rahim's *Pathway to Arabic* textbook series.
