# Selections from the Glorious Quran — Study Sessions

Welcome to the study notes for Selections from the Glorious Quran. Below you will find the links to the individual study session notes, which cover the translation, tafsir, vocabulary, and Arabic grammar lessons derived from selected Quranic passages.
