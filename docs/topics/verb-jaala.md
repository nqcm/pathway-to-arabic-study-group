# جَعَلَ — Four Meanings

**جَعَلَ / يَجعَلُ** is one of the most versatile Arabic verbs, carrying four distinct grammatical meanings that determine the sentence structure.

---

## Meaning 1: فِعل التَّحوِيل — Physical Transformation

**Meaning**: to take something and physically transform it into something else.

**Structure**: takes **two mafāʿīl bih** — what was originally the mubtadaʾ becomes mafʿūl bih #1, what was the khabar becomes mafʿūl bih #2.

> *جَعَلتُ الدَّارَ مَدرَسَةً* — "I turned the house into a school"
> - الدَّار = mafʿūl bih #1
> - مَدرَسَةً = mafʿūl bih #2

### Quranic Examples

> *وَجَعَلنَا الشَّمسَ سِرَاجًا* — "We made the sun a lamp" (Nūḥ 71:16)
> *وَجَعَلنَاكُم شُعُوبًا وَقَبَائِلَ* (Al-Ḥujurāt 49:13) — "We made you into peoples and tribes"

---

## Meaning 2: أَفعَال القُلُوب — Mental Assumption

**Meaning**: to think/believe/assume that something is something — no physical change, only mental.

**Structure**: same two mafāʿīl structure as Meaning 1.

### Quranic Example

> *وَجَعَلُوا المَلَائِكَةَ إِنَاثًا* — "They believed/made the angels [to be] female"
> - المَلَائِكَةَ = mafʿūl bih #1
> - إِنَاثًا = mafʿūl bih #2

---

## Meaning 3: فَعَل الشُّرُوع — Commencement

**Meaning**: to start/begin doing something — like the kāna-type nāqiṣ verbs.

**Structure**: acts like كَانَ:
- **ism** (marfūʿ) + **khabar** (jumlah fiʿliyya with muḍāriʿ)

> *جَعَلَ حَامِدٌ يَضرِبُنِي* — "Ḥāmid started hitting me"
> - حَامِدٌ = ism (marfūʿ)
> - يَضرِبُنِي = khabar (jumlah fiʿliyya — always muḍāriʿ, not māḍī)

!!! important "Khabar Is Always Muḍāriʿ"
    This distinguishes جَعَلَ (Meaning 3) from كَانَ — the khabar must be a verbal sentence containing a muḍāriʿ verb.

---

## Meaning 4: فِعل تَامّ — Simple Creation

**Meaning**: to create, to make simply — no transformation, no mental assumption, no commencement.

**Structure**: normal tāmm verb — takes one mafʿūl bih only.

### Quranic Example

> *الحَمدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي جَعَلَ لَنَا اللَّيلَ وَالنَّهَارَ* — "Praise be to Allah who made the night and the day for us"
> - اللَّيلَ = mafʿūl bih (single)

---

## Summary

| Meaning | Arabic Term | Mafāʿīl | Key Feature |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | فِعل التَّحوِيل | Two | Physical transformation |
| **2** | أَفعَال القُلُوب | Two | Mental only, no physical change |
| **3** | فَعَل الشُّرُوع | Ism + khabar | Commencement; khabar always muḍāriʿ |
| **4** | فِعل تَامّ | One | Simple creation/making |
