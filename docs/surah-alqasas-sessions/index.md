# At the Well of Madyan

A series studying selected āyāt from **Sūrat al-Qaṣaṣ** (سُورَة الْقَصَص), focusing on the story of Mūsā (AS) at the well of Madyan (Qasas 23–28). The lessons blend tafseer, Arabic grammar, morphology, and cultural context, using Dr. Abdur Rahim's *Pathway to Arabic* textbook series.

---

## Study Sessions

- [Session 1](session-1.md) — Sūrat al-Qaṣaṣ intro; Qaṣaṣ vs. Anbāʾ al-Rusul; Āyah 23 — arriving at the well; لَمَّا two types
- [Session 2](session-2.md) — ذَادَ (defend/drive away); جُمَل positions in the sentence; ثُمَّ vs. فَ; لَام الجَار intro; مِن البيانية
- [Session 3](session-3.md) — Six meanings of لَام الجَار; Āyah 25 — girl with حَيَاء; حَال المتعدد; القَوِيّ الأَمِين
- [Session 4](session-4.md) — Hollow verb conjugation (أجوف); Āyah 27 — marriage agreement; numbers as ẓarf; أَجَل root; دَيْن/دِين
- [Session 5](session-5.md) — Defective verbs (ناقص); Āyah 29 — the fire at night; آنَسَ; جَذوَة; تَنزِيل vs. إِنزَال
- [Session 6](session-6.md) — Root ص-ل-ي; passive نُودِيَ; اسم الجنس الجمعي; four types of أَن; root ج-ن-ن
- [Session 7](session-7.md) — ضَمِير الشَّأن; أَن المخففة intro; Āyāt 31–34; جَوَاب الأمر; رَهَب vocabulary
- [Session 8](session-8.md) — أَن المخففة completion; Āyāt 33–37 — Mūsā's plea, Hārūn, هَامَان; مِن الزائدة
- [Session 9](session-9.md) — Detached vs. attached pronouns; لَعَلَّ; Āyāt 38–43 — Pharaoh cast into the sea; يَمّ; جَعَلَ with two mafāʿīl; قُرُون
